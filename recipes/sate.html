Resep Sate Madura

Description

Kali ini kita akan membuat sate dengan menggunakan resep legendaris nan magis.

Satay Ingredients

  • 600 gram of chicken thigh meat, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 4 tablespoon of sweet soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon of vegetable oil
  • 25 bamboo skewers

Peanut Sauce Ingredients

  • 250 gram of roasted peanut
  • 3 candlenuts
  • 6 red cayenne chilies
  • 4 tablespoon of coconut palm sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 600 ml of water

How to Make:

  1. Grind the peanuts, candlenuts, and chilies in a blender or food processor.
  2. When the paste is smooth, transfer it to a sauce pan, add the rest of the peanut sauce ingredients, and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and let the sauce simmer until it thickens.
  4. Turn off the heat and set aside.
  5. Mix chicken, sweet soy sauce, oil, and 150 grams of peanut sauce in a bowl and marinate for 30 minutes.
  6. Then thread the chicken pieces on bamboo skewers.
  7. Grill the skewers until cooked or slightly charred, basting with marinating sauce as required.

Serve the Sate Madura with peanut sauce.
